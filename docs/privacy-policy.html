Privacy Policy — Kenza Call

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Kenza Call ("the app") is a personal calling-experience app that connects you to a conversational AI voice agent. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

Information we process

  • Microphone audio (your voice). While you are on a call, the app captures audio from your microphone and streams it in real time to ElevenLabs, our conversational-AI provider, so the agent can hear and respond to you.
  • Conversation content. Your spoken words are transcribed and processed by ElevenLabs and its configured large-language-model provider to generate replies.
  • Configuration you enter. The agent identifier and optional API key you enter in Settings are stored only on your device (in local app storage).

What we do NOT collect

  • The app does not run its own servers and does not store your audio, transcripts, or conversations on any server we control.
  • The app does not collect names, contacts, location, advertising identifiers, or analytics.
  • The app contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Third-party processing

Voice audio and conversation content are sent to ElevenLabs for speech recognition, language understanding, and voice synthesis. ElevenLabs (and the language-model provider configured for the agent) process this data under their own privacy terms. Please review the ElevenLabs Privacy Policy.

Microphone permission

The RECORD_AUDIO permission is used solely to enable the live, two-way voice conversation during a call. Audio is not recorded to a file or retained by the app after the call ends.

Voice cloning & consent

The voice used by the agent is configured by the app owner in ElevenLabs. Cloned voices must only be created and used with the explicit consent of the person whose voice it is. This app is intended for personal use with that consent and must not be used to impersonate or deceive anyone.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and should not be used by them.

Data retention & deletion

Settings stored on your device are removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage. To request deletion of any data held by ElevenLabs, contact ElevenLabs directly.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
