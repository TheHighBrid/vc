## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Kenza Call recreates the familiar phone-call experience and connects it to a
live, natural-sounding AI voice you can actually talk to.

Place a call or answer an incoming one, then have a real, back-and-forth spoken
conversation — the AI listens, understands the context, and replies instantly in
a warm, lifelike voice.

FEATURES
• Classic full-screen call experience — incoming-call ringtone and vibration,
  answer/decline, and an in-call screen with a live timer.
• Familiar dial pad with key tones.
• In-call controls: mute, speaker, and keypad.
• Live two-way voice powered by ElevenLabs Conversational AI — fast, low-latency
  replies with high realism.
• Natural interruptions: start talking and the voice pauses to listen, just like
  a real call.
• Simple setup: add your agent in Settings and you're ready.

ABOUT THE VOICE
The conversational voice is configured by you in ElevenLabs. Cloned voices must
only be used with the consent of the person whose voice it is. This app is for
personal use with that consent — never to impersonate or deceive others.

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 8.0 or newer.
• Microphone permission (used only during a call for the live conversation).
• An ElevenLabs Conversational AI agent (set up once in the app's Settings).

Your audio is streamed to ElevenLabs to power the conversation and is not stored
by the app. See our privacy policy for details.
```
